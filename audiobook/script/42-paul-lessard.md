# Paul Lessard

Paul and Anna Marie were married June 3, 1978. Their children are **Sheena Mary** born January 11, 1980 and **Marc Raymond** born September 10, 1982.

In early October 1983, they moved to Winnipeg where Paul is Executive Director for Manitoba Labour Education Centre. Anna Marie works as a technician for Xerox Canada. Sheena is in Grade 2.
